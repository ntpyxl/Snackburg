OUR STORY

Snackburg started as a weekend backyard grilling hobby. As our founder, Ramon, was doing this every weekend, he'd make adjustment to his patty recipe every week in order to further innovate his recipe. On one fortunate day, he decided that he had deemed his recipe perfect to be shared to other hamburger enjoyer. He then decided to also add snacks such as fries and chips into the mix to add as an appetizer to the wonderful burgers.

WHAT'S NEXT?

Until this day, we still continue to improve our products altogether along with our service to provide you exceptional quality snack and burgers. From the bottom of our hearts, we will never stop on listening to your feedbacks as we continue to innovate products that will keep on leaving you in awe!
